Quick answer

Most virtual event failures are preventable. They usually come from missing rehearsals, unclear backstage ownership, weak backup links, and platform assumptions that were never tested.

West Peek prevention system

  • Speaker onboarding and technical checks
  • StreamYard broadcast workflow review
  • LiveKit venue and permission checks where relevant
  • Daily backup room readiness
  • Run-of-show cue review
  • Moderator and producer escalation paths
