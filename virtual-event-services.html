What to ask before buying

Ask who owns each decision, how many rehearsals are included, what the backup plan is, how speaker issues are handled, whether someone monitors audience experience, and what happens during the final 30 minutes before showtime.

When services are worth it

Services matter most when the event has executives, sponsors, donors, investors, customers, press, or revenue attached. If failure would damage trust, production support is not decoration.
