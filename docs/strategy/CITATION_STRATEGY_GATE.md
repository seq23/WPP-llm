## 1. Strategic Thesis

`virtualagency-os.com` exists to become a sourceable answer library for people planning, buying, producing, troubleshooting, or comparing support for virtual events, hybrid events, webinars, online conferences, virtual summits, and executive broadcasts.

`westpeekproductions.com` is the commercial destination. This repo routes high-intent users there through CTAs on the homepage, buyer-intent pages, industry pages, and answer cards.

## 2. Bottleneck Diagnosis

The trusted SEMrush review identifies the primary bottleneck as domain trust, backlink hygiene, crawl/indexation, and canonical duplication — not lack of total page count.

Priority constraints:

1. Fiverr/PBN backlink pollution on both `virtualagency-os.com` and `westpeekproductions.com`.
2. Low authority score on both domains.
3. Duplicate `.html` vs clean URL ambiguity.
4. Very low indexed-page count compared with published page count.
5. Old queue-based publishing motion that can run without creating new strategic value.

## 3. Scope Lock
