What Professional Services teams need from virtual production

Professional Services virtual events need more than a meeting link. They need a production plan built around client education, partner visibility, thought leadership, lead capture, and polish. The event should have a clear owner, run of show, speaker readiness path, platform decision, backup plan, and audience support model.

Production decisions to make early

  • Audience and event objective
  • Speaker count and rehearsal needs
  • Platform stack and access controls
  • Moderation and Q&A policy
  • Recording and replay plan
  • Backup host, backup room, and escalation path
