Virtual Event Marketing

Virtual event marketing should increase qualified attendance without creating promises the production system cannot support.

Get production helpEmail scooter@westpeek.ventures

Marketing-production alignment

Marketing owns the audience promise. Production owns the live reality. Those teams need one shared source of truth for agenda, speakers, access links, sponsor promises, registration emails, reminder timing, and post-event assets.

Promotion checklist

  • Audience segment and offer
  • Registration page
  • Speaker announcement copy
  • Email reminder sequence
  • LinkedIn/social assets
  • Sponsor promo kit
  • Calendar invite copy
  • Replay and follow-up plan

Risk control

Never promote features, speakers, giveaways, sponsor activations, or interactive segments that have not been confirmed in the run of show.

Send the event type, target date, audience size, and must-not-fail moments. West Peek Productions will route the conversation from there.
